About Us

BTFC was started 7 years ago by Mrs Anju Abbott and her husband Sandeep Abbott. Their son Pushkin was a football fanatic and used to go to Jawaharlal Nehru stadium to train. The journey was not only tiresome but soon the commute started to become a problem. To them the Antaranga ground, which then had no grass or other facilities, seemed a suitable place to get pushkin personally coached. After meeting some Nigerian coaches, they finally started the 1x1 sessions for Pushkin with coach Kellichi. After seeing some serious football being played in the ground, kids from all the nearby societies gathered around and wanted to join the training. Never having thought of starting an academy, the duo liked the idea. They decided to charge a nominal fee from the players which will be used towards maintaining the ground and paying the coaches. Since then Black tigers has touched many new heights of success. Consequently winning awards in tournaments all around Delhi.
